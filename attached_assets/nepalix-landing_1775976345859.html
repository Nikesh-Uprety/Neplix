The Problem

Nepal's commerce is
broken by design

Every Nepali merchant faces the same 4 walls. NEPALIX tears them all down.

Fragmentation
10 tools, 0 visibility
Merchants juggle WhatsApp orders, Excel inventory, separate POS, and multiple payment apps. No single source of truth — costing 30% of potential revenue every month.
Payment chaos
eSewa, Khalti, Cash — everywhere
Nepal has 8+ payment methods and no unified layer. Customers abandon checkouts because their preferred gateway isn't supported. Every missed payment is a lost sale.
Logistics hell
No delivery infrastructure

Flying blind
Zero analytics, zero growth
Most Nepali businesses have no idea which products are profitable, which customers churn, or when their peak hours are. Decisions are gut-feel, not data-driven.
